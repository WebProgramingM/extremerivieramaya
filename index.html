Our goal here at Extreme Riviera Maya is to offer the world all the extreme sports and activities that Mexico’s Riviera Maya has to offer.... and believe us, there are plenty of options!
For those that are unfamiliar, the Riviera Maya includes a large portion of Mexico’s Yucatán Peninsula that includes the greater areas of Cancun, Playa del Carmen, Tulum, extending all the way down to Belize... essentially the entire state of Quintana Roo, Mexico.
From some of the worlds’ best beaches to Ancient Maya Ruins there are endless opportunities to satisfy your inner need for adrenaline pumping activities! Whether it be cave diving/snorkeling within the worlds’ most extensive underground network of rivers, lakes, Cenotes and reefs to soaking up the breathtaking views of the ancient coastal jungle while Sky Diving from high above Playa del Carmen... there is sure to be something for everyone!
